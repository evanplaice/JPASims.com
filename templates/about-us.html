JPA is an International Simulator Consulting and Technical Support company headquartered in Westminster, Colorado, USA. JPA has been assisting customers throughout the Commercial Aviation industry for over 10 years. As a small, 100% veteran owned business, we can offer tailored large or small scale support.
Philosophy
JPA provides only the services a simulator operator or owner requires from the small consulting role up to the total turnkey solution. In some cases, companies only wish the expert advice from JPA when considering purchasing a simulator and bringing it into training. In others, JPA has assisted customers from the beginning of the search for a training device, through purchase of the device, relocation, facility design, setting up the simulator operation, customizing the simulator to required training needs and certifying authority standards. JPA has even recruited and trained the staff in order for the simulator operation to get off to a good start. In other cases JPA has only assisted with some minor segment of the entire process.
The guiding philosophy is to offer the simulator operator only the services they really need. We can adapt our products and services to whatever the need may be. Our overhead is extremely low, so little is spent and charged to the customer for marketing, business management, etc. Our resources go 90% directly into the product we deliver to the customer. Assistance with buying or selling simulators and other flight training equipment is provided either in a Seller-Broker or as a Buyer Representative role. The relationship is always structured to meet the situation.
